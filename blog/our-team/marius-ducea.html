Marius Ducea is a Systems Administrator for Promet. He has been working for the company since 2006. Marius has more than 10 years of experience in the ISP and web hosting field and he specializes in building and managing large-scale systems and application architectures using open source platforms. Lately he been very interested in systems automation and spends a lot of time with modern configuration management tools like Puppet and Chef. When not working, he likes to spend as much time as possible with his family.
