--- layout: team_member title: Brant Wynn author: brant-wynn footer: false --- Brant Wynn Solutions Architect / Developer Brant Wynn is a Solutions Architect at Promet Source. He has worked on numerous Drupal projects involving everything from rapid prototyping for small start-ups to large-scale configurations for multinational corporations. During his time at Promet he has helped forge processes that ensure productivity without sacrificing developer creativity or client expectations. Brant is a co-maintainer of the Bean module on Drupal.org and is an advocate for bicycle safety and awareness in the city of Chicago.
